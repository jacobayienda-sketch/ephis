Environmental Public Health Information System (EPHIS)

Welcome to EPHIS

A unified system for managing environmental and public health data across Elgeyo Marakwet County. Access reliable insights, monitor compliance, and promote a healthier community.

Staff Login Public Reports

Core Modules

Complaints & Incidents

Submit and track environmental or public health complaints.

Premises Management

Registration and inspection of business and health premises.

Inspections

Risk-based inspections for compliance and quality assurance.

Food Control

Ensure food safety and hygiene standards in all establishments.

Water Quality Monitoring

Monitor safety of water sources and track laboratory results.

Waste Management

Track waste collection, disposal, and enforcement actions.

Vector & Vermin Control

Map high-risk areas and coordinate vector control interventions.

About the System

EPHIS is developed under the Department of Health Services, County Government of Elgeyo Marakwet, to strengthen public health surveillance, improve service delivery, and enable data-driven decision making at all levels.
