About the Platform

EPHIS is a comprehensive county-wide digital solution designed to enhance public health service delivery and environmental health protection in Kenya.

Through this platform, residents can easily report health and environmental concerns, submit evidence through photos and documents, and track the resolution process from start to finish. Our system ensures transparency and accountability in addressing community health issues.

The platform automatically routes complaints to the relevant county health officers based on location and issue type, ensuring swift action and efficient resource allocation.
